## Description of Service

Buffr is a personal productivity app that helps you track your office attendance against your employer's return-to-office (RTO) policy. The App provides:
- Day-by-day logging of work location (office, work from home, vacation, sick, holiday)
- Calculation of your current policy adherence and "flex days"
- Forecasting of future compliance based on your schedule
- Optional iCloud sync across your Apple devices
